About Me

Hi, I am Kavya Datchayani. I am a passionate full-stack web developer. I started my journey as a web developer in January 2023. It's been almost 9 months, and I have learned a lot of things that are essential for becoming a full-stack web developer. I started with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Node.js, React.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and more. But did I master anything?

No, not really. I only learned about 50% of everything in the course I enrolled in. It's an online course by Angela Yu, and she taught everything quite well. However, after completing every module, I was stuck at the end. I was puzzled and confused about everything I had learned.

After a lot of thought, I realized that all I had done was follow instructions. If I encountered any errors, it wasn't easy to solve them on my own. I felt like I needed help, so I decided to take things slowly and started working on projects based on the languages I had learned. We all need patience in the end to achieve what we really want.

And Yes, I started coding on my own and encountered many errors. It made me learn a lot, and I became pretty good in everything but still i sometimes feel like a mess but i know how to find the solution. Finding Solution, It's all we need to become Full-Stack Web Developer . All I'm saying here is, don't give up quickly. Learn, code, be consistent, repeat, and you will succeed.
